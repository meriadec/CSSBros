Motivation

I'll not try to explain why CSS naming is hard. A lot of people explain this far better than me.

To deal with this problem, you can adopt a naming methodology (BEM, SMACSS, etc.). You can use CSS Modules to avoid naming conflicts by adding hashes into class names. You can also surrender and use inline style (Radium, ReactCSS, etc.). That's not the point here.

CSSBros are just little dudes, who will help you in your most common css patterns. I believe that remembering firstnames is easier than remembering keywords. Meet them, and if you like, adopt them.
